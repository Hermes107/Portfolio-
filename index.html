About Me

My name is Zoe Pearson, and I'm a UX/UI designer passionate about creating meaningful experiences through thoughtful design. Currently studying at James Madison University, I specialize in website & app design, social media design, visual communication, and interactive experiences.

I have many passions but my dream job would be working with the technology and art department of a large company to create a product that is at the forefront of artistic creativity and technological advancements.

I believe in design with purpose, whether it's raising awareness for conservation issues, creating educational content, or developing intuitive user interfaces. My work combines research-driven strategy with creative visual solutions.
